Strategy for filling in missing values. Missing values are to be expected with these device measures as a persons movements my not be determined to be walking by the device algorithms. The other options include more sedentary activities sitting, laying, and standing. Also, the person may not be wearing the device. Given the pattern of some days having very high NAs and the desire to eliminate zeros from the pattern, this missing value strategy first sets the NAs to 1 and then sets the dates with high proportions of NAs to the NA average across observations. First set the 13,000 plus NAs to one.

raw3$steps[is.na(raw3$steps)] <- 1
